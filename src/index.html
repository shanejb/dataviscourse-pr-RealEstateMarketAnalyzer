When you are thinking of buying a house or looking for a place to rent, you may be interested in an analysis of different real estate markets. It can be difficult and confusing to do a comparative analysis of real estate markets. However, Zillow, an online real estate database company, provides a U.S. median home value index (ZHVI) and a rent index (ZRI) with data for 50 states in the United States. According to Zillow, the median rent price in the United States is $1,550 and the median home value in the United States is $191,200 as of October 2016. In 2016 home values in the United States increased by an average of 6.2%, Zillow predicts in the United States that home values will increase by an average of 3% in 2017. The goal of this analyzer is to show readers comparable home and rent values between states, thus providing a general idea and track record of the differences of the real estate markets in different states over the years.
