If you start buying a house, you will concern a real estate market analysis. We need to gather many factors that lead studying a comparative real estate market analysis comes up against difficulties. However, Zillows, an online real estate database company, provides us median home value index (ZHVI) and rent index (ZRI) with data of 50 states in U.S. According to Zillows, the median rent price in the United States is $1,550 and the median home value in the United States is $191,200 by October 2016. Zillows predicts home value will raise 3% next year, which have increased up 6.2% over past year. The goal of the analyzer is to show readers comparable home and rent values between states over years.
